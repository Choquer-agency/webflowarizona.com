**Can Webflow handle the traffic volumes that growing Seattle tech companies experience?**
Yes. Webflow sites are hosted on a global CDN powered by AWS and Fastly, capable of handling millions of visits without performance degradation. Companies like Zendesk, Dell, and Dropbox have used Webflow for their marketing sites at scale. For Seattle businesses experiencing rapid growth, Webflow's infrastructure scales automatically — there are no servers to upgrade or hosting plans to renegotiate.

**Is Webflow a good fit for Seattle businesses that need to integrate with complex tech stacks?**
Webflow integrates with the tools that Seattle tech companies already use. Native integrations cover HubSpot, Google Analytics, and Facebook Pixel. For more complex needs, Webflow's API and tools like Zapier, Make, and Finsweet Attributes extend functionality to connect with Salesforce, Marketo, Segment, and virtually any other platform. The result is a marketing site that fits seamlessly into an existing technology ecosystem.

**How does Webflow compare to building a marketing site in Next.js or Gatsby for Seattle startups?**
Next.js and Gatsby are excellent frameworks for web applications, but they create unnecessary complexity for marketing websites. They require developer involvement for every content update, introduce build processes that slow publishing, and demand ongoing maintenance. Webflow delivers comparable or superior performance for marketing sites while giving non-technical team members full control over content and design. For Seattle startups where engineering bandwidth is the scarcest resource, Webflow is the more pragmatic choice.
