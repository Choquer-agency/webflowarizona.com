## What Should You Look for When Hiring a Canadian Webflow Agency?

Not all agencies that list Webflow on their services page are true Webflow specialists. When evaluating a Canadian Webflow agency, look for these indicators of genuine expertise.

First, check for Webflow certification or partnership status. Certified Webflow partners have demonstrated platform proficiency and maintain a direct relationship with Webflow. This matters when complex technical challenges arise.

Second, review their portfolio for Webflow-specific work. A strong Webflow agency will showcase projects that demonstrate advanced CMS usage, custom interactions, responsive design precision, and performance optimization — not just static page designs.

Third, ask about their process for handoff and training. The best Webflow agencies build sites that clients can manage independently. They provide thorough documentation, recorded training sessions, and ongoing support options. If an agency builds a site that only they can update, that is a red flag.

Finally, evaluate their understanding of Canadian business requirements. Can they discuss PIPEDA compliance confidently? Have they built bilingual sites before? Do they understand Canadian accessibility standards? These questions separate genuine Canadian Webflow expertise from surface-level claims.

## How Does Webflow Support Bilingual Canadian Websites?

Bilingual websites are a reality for many Canadian businesses, whether serving federal government contracts, operating in Quebec, or simply wanting to reach the broadest possible Canadian audience. Webflow provides several approaches to bilingual site architecture.

The most common approach uses Webflow's CMS to create parallel content structures for English and French. Each piece of content has fields for both languages, and the front end displays the appropriate version based on user selection. A Canadian Webflow agency can implement language switchers, localized URLs, and proper hreflang tags to ensure search engines index both versions correctly.

For businesses with more complex multilingual needs, Webflow integrates with localization platforms like Weglot, which automate translation management while maintaining Webflow's visual editing experience. A knowledgeable agency will recommend the approach that best fits the client's content volume, update frequency, and budget.
