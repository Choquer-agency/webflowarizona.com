## How Does Webflow Compare to WordPress for Arizona Companies?

The comparison between Webflow and WordPress matters because roughly 60% of Phoenix businesses that come to us are migrating away from WordPress. Here is what they consistently report after the switch:

**Speed improvements**: Webflow sites load 30-50% faster than comparable WordPress sites. In a market where Phoenix consumers increasingly browse on mobile during their commutes along the I-10 or I-17, that speed difference directly affects bounce rates and conversions.

**Lower maintenance costs**: WordPress requires constant plugin updates, security patches, and hosting management. Webflow handles hosting, SSL, and CDN distribution natively. For a typical Phoenix small business, this eliminates $2,000-$5,000 per year in ongoing maintenance expenses.

**Visual design control**: Webflow's visual builder gives designers pixel-perfect control without writing code, which means Arizona businesses get exactly the site they envisioned — not a theme that "kind of" matches their brand.

**Built-in SEO tools**: From auto-generated sitemaps to clean semantic HTML and customizable meta tags, Webflow provides SEO infrastructure that WordPress only achieves through a stack of third-party plugins.
