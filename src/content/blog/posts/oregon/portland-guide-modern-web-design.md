## How Does Webflow Support Portland's Sustainability Values?

This is where Webflow becomes particularly relevant to Portland's ethos. Websites have a carbon footprint — every page load consumes energy through data transfer, server processing, and device rendering. Bloated WordPress sites with dozens of plugins, unoptimized images, and inefficient code consume significantly more energy per visit than they need to.

Webflow sites are inherently leaner. They load 30-50% faster than equivalent WordPress builds, which means less data transferred and less energy consumed per visit. Our Webflow projects achieve an average Lighthouse performance score of 96, compared to the 55-70 range typical of WordPress. For Portland businesses that value environmental responsibility, a faster and cleaner website is not just a performance advantage — it aligns with their brand promise.

Additionally, Webflow's hosting runs on AWS and Fastly CDN infrastructure with increasingly renewable energy commitments. Portland businesses that care about sustainable operations can feel better about their website's environmental impact.

## How Do Portland Businesses Migrate from WordPress to Webflow?

Migration is one of the most common projects we handle for Portland clients. The process typically follows these steps:

1. **Content audit**: We catalog every page, blog post, and media asset on the existing WordPress site to determine what migrates and what gets retired.
2. **Design and UX overhaul**: Rather than recreating the old site in Webflow, we treat the migration as an opportunity to modernize the design, improve navigation, and optimize for conversions.
3. **CMS structure setup**: Webflow's CMS is more structured than WordPress, which means we define clean content models that make future updates intuitive for the client's team.
4. **Content migration**: Blog posts, images, and other assets are transferred and optimized for Webflow's hosting environment.
5. **301 redirects and SEO preservation**: We map every old URL to its new equivalent to ensure search rankings are preserved throughout the transition.
6. **Launch and monitoring**: After launch, we monitor analytics for 30 days to confirm traffic and ranking stability.

Portland businesses that follow this process have seen an average traffic increase of 47% within six months — driven by faster load times, better Core Web Vitals scores, and improved mobile experience.
