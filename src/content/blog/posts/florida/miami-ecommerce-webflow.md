## How Does Webflow E-Commerce Compare to Shopify for Miami Businesses?

This is the question we answer most often for Miami merchants, and the comparison breaks down along clear lines:

**Design freedom**: Shopify themes constrain layout and interaction design. Webflow provides unlimited design flexibility. For Miami brands where visual identity drives purchasing decisions, this is often the deciding factor.

**Performance**: Webflow E-Commerce sites achieve Lighthouse scores averaging 96 and load 30-50% faster than most Shopify stores, which are often weighed down by app scripts and theme bloat. Faster sites convert better — a 100-millisecond improvement in load time can increase conversions by up to 8%.

**Cost structure**: Shopify charges transaction fees of 0.5-2% on top of monthly subscription costs unless merchants use Shopify Payments. Webflow E-Commerce charges a flat monthly rate with no transaction fees through Stripe integration. For a Miami business doing $50,000 per month in online sales, eliminating transaction fees saves $3,000-$12,000 annually.

**Product limits**: Webflow's Business E-Commerce plan supports up to 500 products. For Miami businesses with larger catalogs, Shopify or a headless commerce solution may be more appropriate. But for the majority of Miami DTC brands with focused product lines, 500 products is more than sufficient.

**Customization without apps**: Shopify merchants typically install 10-20 apps to achieve the functionality they need, each adding cost and page load overhead. Webflow includes most of this functionality natively — custom forms, animations, CMS-driven content, and responsive design — without third-party dependencies.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can Webflow E-Commerce handle international shipping for Miami businesses?**
Yes. Webflow E-Commerce supports multiple shipping zones, calculated shipping rates, and flat-rate shipping rules. For Miami businesses that ship across Latin America and the Caribbean — a common requirement given the city's geographic position and cultural connections — Webflow's shipping configuration handles international zones. For more complex logistics needs, integration with shipping platforms like ShipStation extends capabilities further.

**Is Webflow E-Commerce good for Miami businesses selling services, not products?**
Webflow is excellent for service-based businesses in Miami, but not through the E-Commerce feature specifically. Service businesses benefit from Webflow's standard CMS and form capabilities — building service pages, scheduling integrations through Calendly, and lead capture through custom forms. The E-Commerce feature is specifically designed for physical and digital product sales.

**How does Webflow handle sales tax for Florida e-commerce businesses?**
Webflow E-Commerce includes automatic tax calculation based on the customer's shipping address. For Florida businesses, this means Florida's 6% state sales tax plus applicable county surtaxes are calculated automatically at checkout. Miami-Dade County's 1% discretionary surtax, for example, is handled within Webflow's tax engine. For businesses with complex multi-state tax obligations, integration with tax calculation services like TaxJar provides additional compliance support.
