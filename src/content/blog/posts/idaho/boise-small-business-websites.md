## What Makes Webflow Better Than WordPress for Boise Small Businesses?

The WordPress comparison matters because it is still the default recommendation most Boise web designers make. Here is why that recommendation deserves scrutiny:

**Maintenance burden**: A WordPress site requires monthly updates to the core platform, themes, and plugins. Each update carries a risk of breaking something, which means either the business owner learns to manage WordPress or they pay a developer $100-$200 per month for maintenance. Webflow requires none of this — the platform is maintained by Webflow's engineering team, and updates happen seamlessly.

**Security exposure**: WordPress sites are the most commonly hacked websites on the internet, accounting for over 90% of CMS-related security incidents. Small businesses in Boise rarely have the technical knowledge to implement proper WordPress security, leaving them vulnerable to malware, defacement, and data breaches. Webflow's managed hosting eliminates this attack surface entirely.

**Performance gap**: Our Webflow sites achieve Lighthouse scores averaging 96, while the typical WordPress small business site in Idaho scores between 45 and 65. That performance difference directly affects Google rankings and user experience. Webflow sites load 30-50% faster, which matters in a market where mobile browsing is the primary way Boise customers find local businesses.

**Total cost of ownership**: When you add up hosting ($15-$50/month), security plugins ($100-$300/year), maintenance ($1,200-$2,400/year), and the inevitable emergency fixes, a WordPress site costs a Boise small business $2,500-$5,000 per year to maintain. A Webflow site's hosting costs $14-$39 per month with everything included.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Webflow good for a very small Boise business with just a few pages?**
Absolutely. Webflow is ideal for small Boise businesses that need a focused three to five page website — home, about, services, contact, and perhaps a testimonials page. The platform's clean code and fast performance mean even a simple site will outperform competitors on bloated WordPress installations. For a basic Boise small business site, the investment starts around $3,500, which includes custom design, development, and content population.

**Can I move my existing WordPress site to Webflow?**
Yes. Migrating from WordPress to Webflow is one of the most common projects we handle for Boise businesses. The process includes redesigning the site for Webflow, migrating content and blog posts, setting up 301 redirects to preserve search rankings, and training the business owner on the Webflow Editor. Most WordPress-to-Webflow migrations for small businesses take three to five weeks.

**Do I need to know how to code to use Webflow?**
No. Webflow's Editor interface lets business owners update content, add blog posts, and manage basic site elements without any coding knowledge. The visual builder used for initial design does involve a learning curve, which is why most Boise businesses work with an agency for the initial build and then manage updates independently through the Editor. If you are comfortable using tools like Google Docs or Canva, you will be comfortable using the Webflow Editor.
